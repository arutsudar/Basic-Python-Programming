Average Score
 
A class of 'n' students took a science test. 'x' students had an average (arithmetic mean) score of 'a1'.  The other students had an average score of 'a2'.  Write a program to find the average score of the whole class?
 
Input Format :
Input consists of 4 integers.
The 1st integer corresponds to n.
The 2nd integer corresponds to x.
The 3rd integer corresponds to a1.
The 4th integer corresponds to a2.
 
Output Format :
Output consists of a float that corresponds to the average score.
 
Sample Input :
100
75
25
50
 
Sample Output :
31.25
