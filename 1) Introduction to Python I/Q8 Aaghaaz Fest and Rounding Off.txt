"Aaghaaz" Fest and Rounding Off

The "Aaghaaz" Fest witnessed the participation of more number of students as the day proceeded. One of a non-technical event that sought more attention is the "Striker" event.  In this event the participants should strike 10 bottles by 1 ball, given 3 chances. The number of bottles left after each shot is counted and the average after 3 shots is calculated. If the average is less than 10, the participants win .

Write a program to get the score from the participants and print the same round off to 1, 2, 4 decimal places.
