"Aaghaaz" Fest and Probability

The "Aaghaaz" Fest conducted by the ABC Institute of Technology is a grandiose Annual festival. The Event Co-ordinator of the Fest Rita, had planned to conduct the Fest at Noida.  She figures that there is x% chance that this event will  happen in Noida this year.  If it does, she is y% certain that she will be made the Event Head. What is the probability that Rita will become the Event Head for the Fest at Noida?

Input and Output Format:
Refer sample input and output for formatting specifications.
All float values are displayed correct to 2 decimal places.

Sample Input and Output:
[All text in bold corresponds to input and the rest corresponds to output]

Enter the % of chance that the Fest will be held at Noida
30
Enter the percentage that Rita will be made the Event Head
40
The probability that Rita will be the Event Head at Noida is 0.12
