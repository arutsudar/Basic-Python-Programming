Elections
General Elections for the Country of Gebourneisk are to be held in the month of May and all major parties of the country are working actively to win over the polls and form their Government. The Republic Party is one of the major parties in the country. The leader of the Republic party is a believer of fortune and he is so specific in selecting his election candidates based on their Id. He wanted the Id's of all the contesting candidate of his party to be lucky numbers. Lucky numbers to him are those numbers  in which the sum of all digits are divisible by 8.

Write a program to find all the lucky numbers between two limits 'a' and 'b'(both inclusive) to help the leader make his job simple.  

Input Format:
The first line of the input is an integer which corresponds to the lower limit 'a'.
The second line of the input is an integer which corresponds to the upper limit 'b'. 

Output Format
Print all the integers in each separate line, those numbers in which sum of all the digits are divisible by 8.

Sample Input
10
20

Sample Output
17
